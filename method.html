Development Process

This page provides a basic outline of the typical steps required to bring a new physical product to market. Contact us at the bottom of the page and we can provide you with a personalized proposal of what steps are needed for your specific product and the likely cost of said steps.

Step Zero- Ideation

A client provides Novo with sketches, written/verbal descriptions, and similar products as a baseline starting point for the direction desired for their new product.

Step One- Design

Novo will utilize the materials provided in Step Zero to create three dimensional CAD designs of a client’s product. The client will review the CAD designs and request for a few changes to be made in the next design iteration until they are satisfied.

Step Two- Prototyping

Utilizing the CAD created in Stage One, Novo will create a prototype of the client’s product. Typically these prototypes are hand-made combinations of existing components and 3D printed/custom materials. Usually, after handling the prototype, the client will request for Novo to change the CAD design and create another prototype a few times.

Step Three- Sales Validation

After creating a prototype that the client thinks will WOW customers, the smart client will try to get validation for their new product with sales. This validation will come VIA Novo creating a Kickstarter/Crowdfunding campaign, getting the new product onto shelves in a few stores, creating a simple website, or going to a trade show.

Step Four- Manufacturing

Once the client has validated that the public wants their new product, the client will have Novo prepare to mass manufacture the product. A few changes to the design might be made based on customer feedback and a final design agreed to. Novo will then secure bulk purchasing for all of the off-the-shelf components in a product and create the molds/tooling for any custom components. Clients can then choose to assemble/ship their product themselves, have Novo do it, or have a dropship company handle it.

And just like that,

in a matter of months, a new product will be launched. If the client requires further services like patent preparation, website creation, strategy consulting, sales assistance, quality assurance testing, capital/funding assistance, or a number of other services, Novo can provide these as well.
